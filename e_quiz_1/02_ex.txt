1. !=
This is the "not equals" sign.
It is use to compare one object to another

2. ! before something
Used to append a "not" - evaluated to boolean
Turns into opposite of orignal boolean

3. ! after something
"Bang operator" -
 When used in the context of a method it will modify the object upon which the method is called

4. ? before
The expressions following the ? will be executed depending on whether the expression precceding the ? is true.

5. ? after
Asking whether the preceeding expression evaluates to "true". 
Used in ternary statement.

6. !! before
evaluates the object/expression/etc to true or false.
